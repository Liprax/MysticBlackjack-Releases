# 🧛 Mystic Blackjack

> *A dark, gothic vampire-themed roguelike blackjack game.*

---

## 🎮 About

**Mystic Blackjack** takes the classic card game of Blackjack and transforms it into a dark, high-stakes survival experience. You play as a vampire cursed to gamble with your own Blood Essence — lose it all, and you perish.

Each round, you face off against a dealer in a twisted game of 21. Between rounds, spend your hard-earned HP at the **Ritual Market** to purchase powerful relics and active items that reshape how you play. Build, refine, and corrupt your deck with special card types — Fragile souls that shatter to save you, Negative cards that subtract from your hand, and Common Soul cards that haunt both you and the dealer at once.

Survive long enough and you'll face **Chaos Rounds** — unpredictable modifiers that flip the rules entirely.

---

## ⚔️ Key Features

- 🃏 **Roguelike Deckbuilding** — Banish, transform, and summon cards to craft the perfect deck
- 🛒 **Ritual Market** — Spend HP to buy relics and one-use items each round
- 💀 **Special Card Types** — Fragile, Negative, Reducer, and Common Soul cards
- 🌀 **Chaos Rounds** — Modifiers that change the rules every 10 rounds
- 🏆 **Leaderboard** — Track your longest survival runs
- 🌍 **Bilingual** — Full English & Turkish language support
- 📱 **Mobile UI Mode** — Optimized layout for touchscreen play
